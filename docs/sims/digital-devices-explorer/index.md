# Digital Devices Explorer

Some digital devices collect a lot of personal information about you. Some
collect a little. And some — like a regular toaster — don't collect any
information at all (yet!). Click on each device below to find out.

## How to Use

- **Explore mode** *(default)*: Click any numbered marker on the image — or
  any label in the panel on the right — to see whether that device collects
  personal information and what kind.
- **Quiz mode**: Click the **Quiz** button at the top. You'll be given a
  device name and asked to find it on the image. See how many you can get
  right!

## What's the color code?

| Color | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Red | **Yes** — this device collects personal information |
| Orange | **Some** — it depends on the device or how it's used |
| Green | **Not yet** — this device does not collect personal information |

## Why does this matter?

You will learn a lot more about private and personal information in
**Chapter 5**. For now, the big idea is this: not every device is the same.
The more a device knows about you, the more careful you should be about how
you use it. **Pause, think, act!**
