# References: Building Healthy Tech Habits

1. [Effects of Blue Light on Sleep](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Effects_of_blue_light_technology) - Wikipedia - Explains how blue light from screens affects melatonin production and circadian rhythms, and why limiting screen exposure before bedtime helps children sleep better.

2. [Ergonomics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ergonomics) - Wikipedia - Covers the science of designing workspaces and habits to fit the human body, including proper posture, screen positioning, and the importance of regular movement breaks.

3. [Screen Time](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Screen_time) - Wikipedia - Reviews research on daily screen use among children, including recommendations from health organizations for age-appropriate limits and the role of family media agreements.

4. *The Art of Screen Time: How Your Family Can Balance Digital Media and Real Life* by Anya Kamenetz, PublicAffairs - Practical, research-based advice for families on creating screen time agreements, building tech-free zones, and developing daily habits that keep screens from taking over.

5. *Raising Humans in a Digital World: Helping Kids Build a Healthy Relationship with Technology* by Diana Graber, HarperCollins Leadership - A guide for parents and educators on teaching children to manage their digital lives through habits like mindful use, screen breaks, and family media plans.

6. [Healthy Habits Topic: Media Balance and Well-Being](https://www.commonsense.org/education/digital-citizenship/topic/healthy-habits) - Common Sense Education - A collection of lessons helping students develop healthy relationships with technology through self-awareness, self-management strategies, and understanding how screens affect emotions.

7. [K-5 Family Tips: Healthy Habits](https://www.commonsense.org/education/family-tips/k-5-healthy-habits) - Common Sense Education - Practical family tips for elementary students covering screen breaks, bedtime screen rules, tech-free zones, and building a family media plan together.

8. [The Health Effects of Screen Time](https://www.commonsense.org/education/digital-citizenship/lesson/the-health-effects-of-screen-time) - Common Sense Education - A lesson exploring how screen time affects physical health, particularly sleep, and teaching students to develop responsible media habits through reflection.

9. [NetSmartz Resources for Parents and Educators](https://www.missingkids.org/netsmartz/resources) - National Center for Missing & Exploited Children - Free age-appropriate videos, activity cards, and lesson plans that help families and teachers talk to children about healthy and safe technology use.

10. [Raising Digital Citizens](https://staysafeonline.org/online-safety-privacy-basics/raising-digital-citizens/) - National Cybersecurity Alliance - Guidance for families on building children's healthy digital habits, including open communication strategies, setting boundaries, and modeling good screen behavior.
